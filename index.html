About Us


Founded in early 2017 by Xiaowei Wu, The Racing Parts.inc is well established as one of the best, most reliable online aftermarket retailers in the business. Catering to the needs of all late-model Mustang and F-150 owners and enthusiasts, we sell the hottest products at the best prices you can find. Our staff offers top-notch customer service, both over the phone and in person at our 234,032+ sq.ft. warehouse located just 50 minutes outside of London, ON.

About the Team

Tony Stark

Head Mechanic

I have always been around Mustangs in my professional life, either fixing them as a mechanic, selling them at dealerships, or modding them here at AmericanMuscle. Instead of starting right away with the power mods, I started looking more into suspension and weight reduction. I have always dreamed of entering my Mustang in some type of “cannonball run” so I could put all the finely tuned suspension pieces to work. I hope to eventually swap an IRS into my car along with coil-overs.
